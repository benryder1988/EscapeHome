Welcome to EscapeHome
your guide for DIY escape rooms you can play at home!

Each of our 'EscapeHomes' can be simply set up, and should be able to be completed by one person - so they're perfect for a date night or a game evening with some friends! We try to use items that you would find around your home, and we test each guide on one another before uploading it. We hope you enjoy these as much as we do! We're just starting out with these guides, and will become more creative with practise and feedback :)

Each guide starts with an introduction, the items you will need and how the player should progress through the game. We then have step-by-step instructions on how to set up the EscapeHome, often in the reverse order of how it should be played. For most guides you will at least need a pen, paper, scissors and sellotape, and many will need other items - but feel free to adapt and substitute these to suit what you have! We find that these EscapeHomes work best if you act as a 'game master' for the players, giving hints and helps if needed.
